News & Updates

We have been reaching out to people in terms of basic financial needs and other necessities over the space of two years! Join us as we celebrate Tehilah at 2!

With this celebration and joy in service to humanity, we have also decided to do more. We are happy to announce that Tehilah foundation is now working on supporting students who have passion for music and would love to learn musical instruments and some other languages.

In the nearest future, by God’s grace, Tehilah foundation would be establishing a school of arts that builds talented musicians and those who are passionate about languages. This education would be made as free as possible with certifications valid home and abroad.

Of course, these we would be doing in progressions and expect to grow because we do not despise our tiny little beginnings. In all of these, we do not take our volunteers, sponsors and fundraisers for granted. We appreciate you and we hope to experience more of your generosity and kindness.
